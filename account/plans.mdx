## Free

Get started immediately — no credit card required. Includes 100 market listing calls and 10 insight calls per month. Enough to explore the API, run a few analyses, and evaluate Rekko's quality.

Free tier includes access to:
- Market listings, detail, and price history (LISTING)
- AI analysis, screening, and resolution intelligence (INSIGHT)
- Calibration metrics (always free, no auth required)

## Pro

Full access to all endpoints including trading signals, execution guidance, arbitrage detection, and correlation analysis. Upgrade from the [dashboard](https://rekko.ai/dashboard).

Pro tier adds:
- Trading signals with Kelly-derived sizing (STRATEGY)
- Portfolio-aware strategy with hedge recommendations (STRATEGY)
- Execution guidance with spread analysis (STRATEGY)
- Cross-platform arbitrage scanning (DEEP)
- Correlation analysis (DEEP)
- Webhooks and SSE streaming (DEEP)
- 4x higher rate limits

## Enterprise

Custom quotas, dedicated support, SLA, and priority processing. [Contact us](mailto:dan@rekko.ai) to discuss your needs.

## Upgrading

1. Go to your [dashboard](https://rekko.ai/dashboard)
2. Click **Upgrade to Pro**
3. Complete checkout via Stripe
4. Your key is upgraded instantly — no key change needed

## Downgrading

Access the Stripe billing portal from your dashboard to cancel your Pro subscription. You'll retain Pro access until the end of your billing period, then revert to Free tier limits.
